## 4. Full description

> App Store description max 4,000 chars / Google Play full description max 4,000 chars. The body below is ~1,250 chars and works for both stores.

```
You keep taking screenshots, but you almost never look at them again.
Receipts, appointment messages, coupons, articles you meant to read — they all pile up somewhere in your photo library.

Memsum makes those screenshots useful again.
The name is Memory + Summary, which is exactly what it does.

WHAT MEMSUM DOES

Bring in your screenshots
- Pull a screenshot into Memsum to start organizing it.
- Photo access is used only to read your screenshots.

Read the text inside (OCR)
- Extract the text from an image so it becomes searchable.
- The extracted text is cleaned up for easier reading.

Automatic title, summary, and sorting
- Each capture gets a one-line title and a one-line summary.
- Captures are auto-sorted into 6 groups: Marketing, Events, Receipts, Shopping, Info, and Other — so they are easy to find later.

Events go to Google Calendar
- Memsum detects events that include a date and time.
- When you want, add the event to Google Calendar in a single tap.
- Calendar permission is used only to add events; Memsum does not read your whole calendar.

Weekly 5-line report
- From the captures you collected during the week, Memsum picks 5 worth a second look and shows them in five lines.
- A short way to look back at what you saved that week.

WHO IT'S FOR
- Anyone with hundreds or thousands of screenshots piling up
- People who save appointments, receipts, and info but can never find them again
- People who find opening a separate notes app a hassle

PRIVACY
- Start right away, anonymously — no email or account signup.
- Your Google Calendar connection (token) is kept securely on your device only.
- No ads.

It's okay to forget. Memsum will remind you.
```

- (body length: ~1,250 chars — within 4,000)

## 5. Keywords (App Store Keywords)

- **App Store keywords field** (max 100 chars, comma-separated, no spaces recommended):
  `screenshot,capture,ocr,text,scanner,calendar,event,schedule,summary,recap,receipt,organize,sort`
  - (length: 95 chars — within 100)
  - ASO note: words already used in the app name/subtitle should be dropped from keywords to save the 100-char budget. The list above overlaps a little with the name/subtitle; before final submission, compare against the finalized name/subtitle and remove duplicates.
  - Use singular forms and omit spaces after commas; Apple tokenizes the field, so spaces waste characters.

- **Google Play note**: Play has no separate keywords field. Do ASO by weaving key terms (screenshot, OCR, calendar, event, summary, receipt, organize) naturally into the short and full descriptions. Keyword stuffing violates policy and is not allowed.
